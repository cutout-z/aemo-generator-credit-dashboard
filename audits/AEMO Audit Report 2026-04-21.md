### Root Cause

AEMO operates two separate publication steps:
1. **Final MLF document** (Excel/PDF) — published April 2026. This is what the user confirmed was released.
2. **DUDETAILSUMMARY load** — AEMO adds `START_DATE=2026-07-01` records to MMSDM when the FY takes effect (~July 1).

The MLF Tracker reads exclusively from DUDETAILSUMMARY. The final MLF document is not ingested. Therefore, FY26-27 genuine data will not appear until the July DUDETAILSUMMARY is published.

### Impact

The FY26-27 column is **misleading, not wrong per se** — it shows the MLF currently in effect for each generator. But any changes AEMO has determined for FY26-27 (visible in the published document) are not reflected. Users viewing the tracker will see YOY_CHANGE = 0 for most generators, suggesting no changes when there may be significant ones.
